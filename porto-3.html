Revobank Customer Analysis

Skillset: Python, Customer Segmentation

RevouBank is a bank that based in Europe. They provide a Credit Card that partnering with other company, and one them is RevoShop.

RevoShop is an ecommerce company that has implement the credit card promotion over the last 6 month. Our company got its revenue through interchange fees, which comes at 2.4% per transaction.

The objective: From the last 6 month promotion on RevoShop our company RevouBank are trying to understand the customer behaviour and additionally hoping to reduce the promotion cost.

Insight &Recommendation

  1. Active Spender
    • Targeted Messaging: Analyze the preferences and behavior patterns of this segment to create more personalized and relevant promotional messages. By delivering targeted messages, you can increase engagement and conversion rates, ultimately reducing costs associated with mass marketing campaigns.
    • Optimize Email Campaigns: Segment your email lists to ensure that the right offers and promotions are sent to the appropriate audience. By refining your email targeting, you can minimize costs associated with sending emails to customers who are less likely to respond.
  2. Balanced User
    • Adjust The Promotion: While our average number of transactions and the amount are the highest of all cluster, our profit are the lowest. We can adjust the promotion by either increasing the minimum transaction amount of transaction to use the promo, or reduce the percentage, or limit the promo distribution.
    • Personalized Upselling: Leverage personalized recommendations based on their spending history and preferences to upsell relevant products or services. This targeted approach can increase revenue from existingcustomers while minimizing costs related to broad upselling efforts.
    • Abandon the segmentation: Due to losing money on the average transaction, this cluster probably does not worth the investment and we can just drop the cluster. By doing this we can allocate our resources on the other segmentation.
