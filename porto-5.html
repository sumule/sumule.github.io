Liquor Sales Analysis in Iowa

Skillset: Python, Tableau, SQL, Statistic, EDA

Mangmoty is a retail store specializing in the sale of liquor. Where its goal is to offer competitive prices and a wide variety of liquors.

Objective from this project is to determine most popular liquor brand and category in Iowa. This project also help Mangmoty decide which county in Iowa to open its branch in.

Insight &Recommendation

  1. Tito's Handmade Vodka is widely recognized for having consistently strong sales, positioning itself as a prominent choice in the spirits market. Fireball Cinnamon Whiskey also boasts significant popularity, particularly for its unique flavor profile. Black Velvet, while a notable brand, may not surpass the sales volumes achieved by Tito's Handmade Vodka and Fireball Cinnamon Whiskey.
  2. American Vodkas stand out as the category with the highest sales, showcasing their widespread popularity and consumer preference in the spirits market. While Whiskey Liqueurs and Canadian Whiskies have their own distinct appeal, American Vodkas dominate the sales landscape, reflecting the enduring demand for this particular category.
  3. From all county, top 3 liquor sales in Iowa is in Polk, Linn and Scott, so its best option to open branch in that 3 county.
