Luxura Purchase Behaviour Analysis

Skillset: Spreadsheet, Statistic, EDA

Luxura is facing the core business problem of ineffective marketing strategies in ecommerce. The company needs to identify the current sales performance of each brand and deliver insights and recommendations to help improve sales performance.

What is the promotion strategy that aligns with the current condition?

Those analysis indicates that users might be interested mostly on Celinna.

Based on the histogram, Adibi has relatively the lowest promotion, with most of the promotion around 1-2%.

Recommendation

We learn that Adibi has the lowest amount spent, the lowest promotion rate. Meanwhile, in buyer aspects, we found that users who are not married and have middle to high income have the highest purchase quantity & value, which makes them potential users. Hence, the recommendations are:

  1. Adibi should increase its promotion. Adibi might have the lowest value of orders because it spends low on promotion. Compared to Balena, which has the highest promotion rate, Balena also has a higher purchase value. Hence, partnering with Luxura could be one of the solutions to increase promotion.
  2. Spend personalized services & assistantship for “Not Married” & “Middle to High Income” users, such as special vouchers, specific campaigns, etc., because they might spend more to purchase products.
